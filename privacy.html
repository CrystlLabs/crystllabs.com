Privacy Policy //

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Crystl Labs ("we," "our," or "us") is an independent technology studio dedicated to building proprietary applications. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and website (crystllabs.com).

2. AI Processing and Your Data

Our applications leverage advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide core functionalities. To deliver these features, certain text, image, or contextual data you input into our apps must be transmitted to secure, third-party AI processing providers. Your data is transmitted solely for the purpose of computing within the app. We do not permit our third-party AI providers to use your personal inputs to train their models, and we absolutely do not sell data to brokers.

3. Analytics & Security

We rely on anonymized, aggregated telemetry to architect better frameworks. We implement industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit. Transient data sent for AI processing is discarded immediately after output is generated unless specified otherwise.
