PUBG Drill Instructor pulls your recent PUBG PC matches — kills, damage, placement, time survived — into a dashboard of past deployments. Pick an instructor persona (IRON, SHARP, or NIGHTFALL) and request an AI tactical briefing that tears down your last fight and tells you what to fix. Consent-gated data transmission keeps you in control, and a rewarded ad tops up briefings when you run out. Built for players who want the truth about their gameplay, not another kill-count screen.
