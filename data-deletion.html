Data Deletion Request

In compliance with the Google Play Store and global privacy standards, Crystl Analytics provides a direct method for users to request the complete deletion of their account and any associated personal data generated by our applications.

When you submit a deletion request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account credentials and profile information.
  • Any saved AI-processed outputs tied to your account.
  • Identifiable telemetry or usage history.

*Please note that we cannot recover your data once the deletion process is complete.

How to Request Deletion

Click the button below to send a formal deletion request to our privacy team. Please ensure you email us from the exact email address associated with your app account so we can verify your identity.
