1. Requesting Deletion

In compliance with global privacy standards, we provide a direct method for users to request the complete deletion of their account and any associated personal data generated by our applications.

  • Your account credentials and profile information.
  • Any saved AI-processed outputs tied to your account.
  • Identifiable telemetry or usage history.

WARNING: Data cannot be recovered once deletion is executed.

Click the execute command below to email our privacy team. Please email us from the exact address associated with your app account.

> Execute_Deletion_Request
